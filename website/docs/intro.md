# AI-Native Textbook for Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the interactive textbook for Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics\! This textbook combines traditional educational content with cutting-edge AI features to provide a personalized learning experience.

## Features

- **RAG-Powered Chatbot**: Ask questions about the content and get answers based on the textbook material
- **Personalization**: Content adapts to your background and learning preferences
- **Urdu Translation**: All content available in Urdu for broader accessibility
- **Interactive Elements**: Hands-on exercises and simulations

## Getting Started

1. Navigate through the textbook using the sidebar
2. Use the chatbot widget to ask questions about any topic
3. Toggle personalization based on your background
4. Switch between English and Urdu as needed

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Undergraduate and graduate students in robotics
- Researchers in physical AI and embodied intelligence
- Engineers working on humanoid robotics
- Anyone interested in the intersection of AI and robotics

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming knowledge (Python preferred)
- Understanding of linear algebra and calculus
- Familiarity with robotics concepts is helpful but not required
