# Humanoid Robotics Hardware
This is a placeholder page for the Humanoid Robotics Hardware section. Humanoid robots are complex electromechanical systems that require sophisticated hardware components to mimic human-like form and function.
## Key Components​
  * Actuators and motors for joint movement
  * Sensors for perception and feedback
  * Structural frame and materials
  * Power systems and energy management
  * Control electronics and processing units


## Actuation Systems​
  * Servo motors and actuators
  * Pneumatic and hydraulic systems
  * Series elastic actuators
  * Variable stiffness actuators
  * Biomimetic actuation


## Sensor Systems​
  * Proprioceptive sensors (position, velocity, force)
  * Exteroceptive sensors (cameras, LIDAR, tactile)
  * Inertial measurement units (IMUs)
  * Force/torque sensors
  * Tactile sensing arrays


## Structural Design​
  * Lightweight materials (carbon fiber, advanced polymers)
  * Anthropomorphic proportions
  * Modular design approaches
  * Safety considerations
  * Maintenance accessibility
