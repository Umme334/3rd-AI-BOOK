This is a placeholder page for the Humanoid Control Systems section. Controlling humanoid robots presents unique challenges due to their complex dynamics, multiple degrees of freedom, and need for stable, human-like motion.

  * Real-time control requirements

  * Safety and emergency systems

  * Zero moment point (ZMP) control
  * Capture point theory
  * Whole-body control approaches
  * Model predictive control

  * Inverse kinematics solutions
  * Trajectory optimization
  * Path planning in dynamic environments
