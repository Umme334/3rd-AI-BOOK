# Humanoid Perception Systems
This is a placeholder page for the Humanoid Perception Systems section. Perception is crucial for humanoid robots to understand their environment, interact with objects, and engage with humans effectively.
## Sensory Modalities​
  * Vision systems (stereo, RGB-D cameras)
  * Auditory processing
  * Tactile sensing
  * Proprioception
  * Spatial awareness


## Computer Vision​
  * Object recognition and tracking
  * Human pose estimation
  * Scene understanding
  * Visual SLAM
  * Face recognition and expression analysis


## Auditory Processing​
  * Speech recognition
  * Sound localization
  * Noise filtering
  * Natural language understanding
  * Audio-visual integration


## Multisensory Integration​
  * Sensor fusion techniques
  * Cross-modal learning
  * Attention mechanisms
  * Uncertainty management
  * Real-time processing constraints


## Human-Robot Interaction​
  * Social signal processing
  * Intention recognition
  * Emotional state detection
  * Gaze tracking
  * Gesture recognition
