# Content Personalization
Our textbook adapts to your background and learning preferences to provide a personalized experience.
## How It Works​
  1. We analyze your background in software, hardware, and robotics
  2. Content difficulty is adjusted based on your experience level
  3. Examples and explanations are tailored to your preferences
  4. The system learns from your interactions to improve recommendations


## Toggle Personalization​
Use the personalization toggle to enable or disable this feature based on your needs.
